Azimuth Mix 002 with Guitar

Tue 20 February 2018

This is not my music. I found some cool music by a composer by the name of Mike Mullen. Ever since my first visit to E.P.C.O.T. I have always found a liking toward that dreamy, floating music they would just put in their queues.

If you are standing in line waiting for the ride, they have to enterntain you somehow. Now they have video games and interactive displays. But when I went to E.P.C.O.T. they just had models of space ports and that would just send my imagination running wild. I would ponder about their daily lives, the chores that are required on a space port, how would they entertain themselves and so on.

So as you listen to this, where does your mind go?
