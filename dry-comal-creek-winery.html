Dry Comal Creek Winery

From July 14, 2008: During the 4th of July weekend we visited the Dry Comal Creek Winery. Around the same time, KENS5 in San Antonio visited the winery and did a much better tour/overview in their "Trip on a Tankfull" segment.

Our tour was hosted by Mr. Houser, we got to sample wine right from the vats where they ferment it, and got to sample some gourmet cooking that was paired with their wines.

Our personal favorite is their White-Black Spanish made with the black Spanish grapes grown on their property.
